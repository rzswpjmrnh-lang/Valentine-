One thing I never say out loud

I act like I’m calm, like I’ve got everything figured out… but the truth is, you matter to me more than I ever admit.

You’re the thought that sneaks in when everything gets quiet, the comfort I didn’t know I needed, the reason some days feel lighter just by existing.

I don’t say it enough — or maybe not at all — but I’m really grateful for you. And having you in my life means more to me than you probably realize.
